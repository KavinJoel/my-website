Ingredient

For Chiken Noodles :

  • chicken
  • Noodles
  • Vegetbles
  • oil
  • masala

Instruction

  1. Heat the pan and add some oil.
  2. add the vegetables and stir.
  3. now add masala and stir.
  4. now add chicken and stir.
  5. and finaly add noodle and your chickem noodle is ready.
